ADMIN CORE

SME Assist — Pilots Q3 2026

Admin Core is a coordinated crew of AI agents that runs your back office — HR, finance, contracts, rotas, compliance — entirely on your own infrastructure. Your data never leaves your premises. Every action is auditable. And you don’t need to hire a single new admin person to make it work.

■ HR & people administration

Employee records, onboarding, leave management, and contracts — handled without the filing or the chasing.

■ Finance & expense management

Invoicing, expense tracking and basic accounts. Reduces manual data entry and keeps numbers current.

■ Rotas & scheduling

Staff planning and shift management. Admin Core tracks availability, flags gaps, and suggests cover.

■ Dashboards & reporting

Real-time visibility across the whole back office — one place to see everything that matters.

■ On-premises by design

Runs on your own hardware. Every action is fully auditable by design. Built for regulated sectors.

■ Auditable by design Every action auditable. Data never leaves your premises.
