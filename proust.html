PROUST

Founder Assist — Available now

Proust reads your email, manages your calendar, and briefs you every morning — so you lead, not triage. Set up in ninety seconds. Private by design: calendar and email run inside your Microsoft 365 tenant. Inference is processed in QC’s private EU/UK environment (Managed) or on your own hardware (Appliance) — your data stays in your control.

■Reads your inbox

Prioritises, drafts, and routes. Surfaces the three things you actually need to do today.

■Manages your calendar

Schedules, briefs, and chases. Owns your diary so you don’t have to — and reschedules without losing context.

■Briefs you every morning

Every day at 8 am: what’s urgent, what’s on, who needs you. Read it in sixty seconds with your first coffee.

■Remembers across sessions

Names, decisions, drafts, follow-ups. Never asks the same question twice.

■Stays in your tenant

Runs inside your Microsoft 365. Private by design, audited by default.
